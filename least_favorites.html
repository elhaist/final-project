My Least Favorite Books

These are a few of the books that I have read over the years that I have not enjoyed so much. All of these books have a few things in common:

  • They are all fiction
  • They all failed to keep me engaged and excited

Nineteen Claws and a Black Bird

by Agustina Bazterrica

A collection of nineteen dark, wildly imaginative short stories from the author of the award-winning TikTok sensation Tender Is the Flesh.

It Ends With Us

by Colleen Hoover

“It Ends with Us” follows the protagonist, Lily, who meets Ryle one night on a rooftop. After a few encounters, they begin to get to know one another and fall in love. They had a picture-perfect relationship, but things take a turn when Ryle becomes abusive.

Drive Your Plow Over the Bones of the Dead

by Olga Tokarczuk

In a remote Polish village, Janina devotes the dark winter days to studying astrology and taking care of the summer homes of wealthy Warsaw residents. A neighbor, Big Foot, turns up dead. Soon other bodies are discovered, in increasingly strange circumstances.

Hangman

by Maya Binyam

A man returns home to sub-Saharan Africa after twenty-six years in America. When he arrives, he finds that he does not recognize the country or anyone in it. Thankfully, someone recognizes him, a man who calls him brother—setting him on a quest to find his real brother, who is dying.
